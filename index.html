Relocation

Air conditioning relocation services involve moving an existing air conditioning system from one location to another, including disassembly, transportation, reassembly, and any necessary modifications.

Dismentle

Air conditioning dismantle services involve the disassembly and removal of an existing air conditioning system from a building or location, typically performed when the system is no longer needed or is being replaced.

Repair

Air conditioning repair services involve diagnosing and fixing issues with an existing air conditioning system, such as leaks, broken parts, or other malfunctions, to restore proper functioning and efficiency.

Diagnosis

Air conditioning diagnosis services involve identifying the root cause of issues with an air conditioning system, such as reduced cooling capacity, unusual noises, or increased energy usage, to determine the appropriate course of action for repair or maintenance.

Commercial Services

These services are designed to help commercial customers maintain comfortable and healthy indoor environments in their buildings, while also improving energy efficiency and reducing costs.

Air Conditioner Installation

The installation services involve the installation of new air conditioning systems in commercial buildings, such as offices, retail stores, and restaurants, to provide efficient and reliable cooling for large spaces.

Chiller Installation

Chiller installation services involve the installation of industrial cooling systems called chillers, which are used to cool large commercial or industrial buildings and processes by circulating chilled water throughout the building or equipment.

Cooling Tower

Cooling tower services involve the installation, repair, and maintenance of cooling towers, which are used in industrial processes and HVAC systems to remove excess heat from water and other fluids, helping to ensure efficient operation and prevent overheating.

Electrical Wiring

Electrical wiring services involve the installation, repair, and maintenance of electrical systems in residential, commercial, and industrial settings, including wiring for lighting, power outlets, appliances, and other electrical devices to ensure safe and reliable operation.

A/C Maintanance Q & A

  • 01 Why is scheduled maintance of air conditioning necessary?

    Scheduled maintance of air conditioning is not only necessary to ensure efficient and effective performance of the system, but also for maintaining a healthy and bacteria-free environment. A dirty air conditioning system can accumulate dust, debris, and harmful bacteria, which can compromise the quality of the air circulated in the space. Regular cleaning reduces the risk of bacteria and mold growth, improves indoor air quality, and reduces the likelihood of system breakdowns caused by clogged filters or components.

  • 02 How often should I have my air conditioning system cleaned?

    The frequency of air conditioning cleaning will depend on a variety of factors, including the age and condition of the system, the level of use, and the environment. It is generally recommended to have the air conditioning system cleaned at least once a year.

  • 03 What are the benefits of periodic air conditioning cleaning?

    The benefits of periodic air conditioning cleaning include improved energy efficiency, better indoor air quality, and a longer lifespan for the system. Regular cleaning can also help to prevent breakdowns and reduce the need for repairs.

  • 04 Can I clean my air conditioning system myself?

    While it is possible to clean some parts of an air conditioning system yourself, it is generally recommended to hire a professional service. Professional cleaners have the equipment and expertise needed to ensure a thorough and effective clean, and can also identify and address any potential issues with the system.

  • 05 What are some signs that my air conditioning system needs cleaning?

    Some signs that your air conditioning system may need cleaning include reduced cooling efficiency, strange smells or odors, and increased noise levels. If you notice any of these issues, it is a good idea to have your air conditioning system inspected and cleaned by a professional service.
